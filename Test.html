Frequently Asked Questions

Is Saarthi Print Portal a government website?
No. Saarthi Print Portal is an independent digital print portal, not affiliated with any government body. It works directly through mobile number and OTP-based verification.
What services does Saarthi Print Portal offer?
Voter ID related services, Aadhaar related services, PAN Card related services, and other digital services for retailers and distributors.
How do I log in to Saarthi Print Portal?
Log in using your registered mobile number and password. No government login or portal credentials are required.
Who can use Saarthi Print Portal?
Retailers and distributors across India can register and use Saarthi Print Portal to access Voter ID, Aadhaar, PAN Card and other digital services.

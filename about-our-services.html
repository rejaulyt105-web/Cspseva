How Saarthi Print Portal works

01

Register

Sign up with your mobile number, name and email, then choose Retailer or Distributor based on your business scale.

02

Activate your account

Pay a one-time registration fee automatically online, or manually via UPI reference submission.

03

Load your wallet

Add balance to your in-platform wallet. Every request deducts only the exact listed price — nothing more.

04

Submit a request

Pick a service from your dashboard, fill in the details, and submit it for processing.

05

Track & get support

Follow each request's status — pending, success or failed — and raise a complaint with your order ID or UTR if needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Before you register

Is Saarthi Print Portal a government website?

No. It is an independent digital print portal, not affiliated with the Election Commission of India, UIDAI, the Income Tax Department, or any other government body. Every request works through your registered mobile number and OTP-based verification.

What services does Saarthi Print Portal offer?

Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, AEPS, BBPS, FASTag, recharge, DMT, mini statement, passbook printing, insurance, PM Kisan, birth/death/income/caste/domicile certificates, driving licence, RC, traffic challan, passport assistance, and Udyam, GST and MSME registration assistance — with more added regularly.

Who can register on Saarthi Print Portal?

Anyone looking to operate as a Retailer or Distributor can register, subject to completing the registration process and paying the applicable one-time registration fee.

How do I add money to my wallet?

Instantly through the integrated digital payment option, or manually by submitting a UPI transaction reference for review.

What happens if my service request fails?

If a request is marked failed by our team, the amount deducted for that service is automatically refunded to your wallet.

Is my data safe on Saarthi Print Portal?

Yes. We use secure authentication, encrypted data storage practices and strict access controls to protect your account and transaction data.

About Us

Built so every retailer can run a complete digital services counter

Saarthi Print Portal brings Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, AEPS, BBPS, FASTag, recharge, money transfer, certificates, licences and more onto one dashboard — so retailers and distributors across India don't have to juggle a dozen separate logins to serve their community.

Saarthi Print Portal is an independent digital print portal, not a government website. We are not affiliated with UIDAI, the Election Commission of India, the Income Tax Department, or any other government body. Every request is verified through the customer's own OTP.
Our Story

Why we built Saarthi Print Portal

Across small towns and neighbourhoods in India, local retailers have become the everyday bridge between citizens and essential digital services — helping someone update an Aadhaar detail, print a certificate, or pay a utility bill without a long trip to a government office.

But running that counter usually meant separate logins, separate wallets, and separate learning curves for every single service. Saarthi Print Portal was built to remove that friction: one registration, one wallet, one dashboard, and a clear price for every request before it's submitted.

We built the platform around three simple ideas — transparency in pricing, verification through the customer's own OTP for every sensitive request, and fast, dependable processing that retailers can count on in front of their customers.

What Saarthi Print Portal is not:

It is not a government portal, and it does not offer any way to bypass OTP verification or official identity checks. Every Aadhaar, PAN and Voter ID action on our platform is tied to the account holder's own verified mobile number — that's a line we don't cross, for any request.

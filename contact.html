Frequently Asked Questions

Before you reach out

Is the WhatsApp Channel the official support channel of Saarthi Print Portal?

Yes. The channel linked on this page is the official Saarthi Print Portal WhatsApp Channel for updates, announcements and support guidance.

Will Saarthi Print Portal ever ask for my OTP or password on WhatsApp?

No. We never ask for your OTP, account password or wallet PIN over WhatsApp or any other channel. Please do not share these with anyone claiming to be from our team.

What should I keep ready before messaging for help?

For request-specific issues, keep your order ID or payment UTR ready. For account issues, keep your registered mobile number handy.

Is joining the WhatsApp Channel free?

Yes, joining the channel is completely free and open to all registered retailers and distributors, as well as new users exploring the platform.

Can I get help for every service from this one channel?

Yes. The channel covers registration, wallet, Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, AEPS, BBPS, FASTag, certificates, licences and every other service listed on Saarthi Print Portal.

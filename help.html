Topic 01

Getting Started

How do I register on Saarthi Print Portal?

Sign up with your mobile number, name and email, then choose whether you want to register as a Retailer or a Distributor based on your business scale.

Do I need a government portal login to use Saarthi Print Portal?

No. You use your own mobile number and password on Saarthi Print Portal — there is no dependency on separate government portal credentials.

Is there a registration fee?

Yes, a one-time registration fee activates your account. It can be paid automatically online or manually via UPI reference submission.

What's the difference between a Retailer and a Distributor account?

A Retailer runs a single service counter for their own customers. A Distributor manages and supports a network of retailers within their area.

Topic 02

Wallet & Payments

How do I add money to my wallet?

Instantly through the integrated digital payment option, or manually by submitting a UPI transaction reference for review.

How much does each service cost?

Every service has a clearly listed price shown before you submit a request — there are no hidden charges.

What happens to my wallet balance if a request fails?

If a submitted request is marked failed by our team, the amount deducted for that service is automatically refunded to your wallet.

Is my payment information stored on the platform?

No. We do not store payment card details. All payment processing runs through secure, PCI-compliant infrastructure.

Topic 03

Service Requests

How do I submit an Aadhaar, PAN or Voter ID request?

From your dashboard, select the relevant service category, fill in the required customer details, and submit — every sensitive request is verified with the customer's own OTP.

Where can I track the status of a request?

Every request has its own status — pending, success or failed — visible in that service's history section, along with the date and time of submission.

What services can I offer through Saarthi Print Portal?

Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, AEPS, BBPS, FASTag, recharge, DMT, mini statement, passbook printing, insurance, PM Kisan, certificates, driving licence, RC, traffic challan, passport assistance, and Udyam, GST and MSME registration assistance. See the full Services page for details.

Can I offer AEPS, BBPS and FASTag from the same dashboard?

Yes. AEPS cash withdrawal and mini statements, BBPS bill payments, and FASTag issuance or recharge are all available from the same dashboard as your other services.

Topic 04

Troubleshooting & Complaints

My request shows as failed — what do I do?

Failed requests are automatically refunded to your wallet. If the refund doesn't reflect, raise a complaint from your dashboard using the order ID or payment UTR.

How do I raise a complaint?

Go to the complaint section on your dashboard, reference the relevant order ID or payment UTR, and track the resolution status directly.

My wallet top-up isn't reflecting — who do I contact?

Message us on our official WhatsApp Channel with your UPI reference number, and our team will help you resolve it.

Topic 05

Security & Safety

Is Saarthi Print Portal a government website?

No. Saarthi Print Portal is an independent digital print portal, not affiliated with UIDAI, the Election Commission of India, the Income Tax Department, or any other government body.

Will anyone from Saarthi Print Portal ask for my OTP or password?

No. We never ask for your OTP, account password or wallet PIN over WhatsApp, phone, or any other channel. Please never share these with anyone.

Is my data safe on Saarthi Print Portal?

Yes. We use secure authentication, encrypted data storage practices and strict access controls to protect your account and transaction data.

Does the platform bypass any official verification steps?

No. We do not support, advertise, or provide any method to bypass OTP verification or any other official identity-verification mechanism, for any service.
